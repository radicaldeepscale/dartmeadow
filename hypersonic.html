System Components

Fore Aerospike

Forward aerospike geometry manages shockwave formation at hypersonic velocities, channeling thermal energy into the edge cooling system.

Aft Aerospike

Rear aerospike completes the dual-spike configuration for optimized pressure distribution across the flight envelope.

DART Hypersonic Edge Alloy engineered for extreme thermal resilience at sustained hypersonic edge temperatures.

Electron Current Flow
