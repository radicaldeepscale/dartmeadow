EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
Mission Overview
This report analyzes the potential expansion of human civilization from Earth to the Milky Way and eventually to a new galaxy. The analysis contrasts the hard deadlines of Earth's habitability against the timeline of the Milky Way–Andromeda merger.

The primary threat to humanity is not the galaxy collision (4.5 billion years away), but the aging of our Sun. As solar luminosity increases ~10% over the next 600M–1B years, Earth's oceans will evaporate. This gives humanity a "Golden Era" of approximately 1 billion years to saturate the Milky Way — more than enough time to colonize every viable planet in our galaxy using generation ship technology.
